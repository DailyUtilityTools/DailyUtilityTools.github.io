Expert Insight

Mach Numbers: The speed of sound changes with temperature/altitude.

  • Sea Level: ~761 mph.
  • 30,000 ft: ~678 mph. Mach isn't a fixed speed; it's a ratio relative to the local speed of sound.

Why this matters

Driving in a foreign country? Mixing up 50 mph and 50 km/h gets you honked at (too slow) or pulled over (too fast). In aviation, mixing up Knots and KPH can be fatal.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the speed of light? 299,792,458 m/s. (Or ~670 Million mph). Nothing is faster.

Is a Knot faster than a Mile per Hour? Yes. 1 Knot = 1.15 mph. A cruise ship doing 20 knots is moving at 23 mph.

How fast is a sneeze? Approx 100 mph (160 km/h).
