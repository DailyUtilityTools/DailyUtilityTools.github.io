Expert Insight

Cervical Mucus: Don't just trust the calendar. Look for "Egg White" mucus (clear, stretchy). If you see that, you are fertile right now, regardless of what the app says.

Why this matters

Conception is a numbers game. Even with perfect timing, there's only a ~20-30% chance per month. Hitting the exact window maximizes your odds and minimizes stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this effectively for birth control? No. The "Rhythm Method" has a high failure rate (~24%). Stress or travel can shift your ovulation by days, and sperm are patient. Do not gamble.

What if my cycle is irregular? This calculator assumes regularity. If your cycle swings by 7+ days, use Ovulation Predictor Kits (pee strips) instead.

Does stress delay ovulation? Yes. Stress hormones (cortisol) can tell your ovaries to "hold fire" because it's not a safe time to reproduce.
