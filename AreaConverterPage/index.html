Expert Insight

Gross vs. Net Area: In real estate, "Gross Area" includes walls, elevator shafts, and columns. "Net Area" (or Carpet Area) is the space you can actually walk on. In some buildings, the usable space is 20% less than the advertised gross area! Paint coverage: A standard gallon of paint covers 400 sq ft. Always round up area calculations by 10% for waste and mistakes.

Why this matters

Ordering flooring? If you measure 100 sq ft exactly and buy 100 sq ft of tile, you will run out (because of cuts and breakage). Knowing how to calculate area + buffers saves you a trip to Home Depot.
