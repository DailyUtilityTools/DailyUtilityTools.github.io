Frequently Asked Questions

What's the tip for takeout? Usually 10-15%. Someone still had to pack it, check the sides, and bag it up.

What about bad service? Before tipping 0%, check: Is the kitchen slow? Is the place understaffed? If it's not the waiter's fault, don't punish them. If they were actually rude, 10% sends a message.

Do I tip on alcohol? Yes. Bartenders work hard too. $1-2 per drink or 20% of the tab.
