Expert Insight

Sabermetrics vs. Traditional: Old school scouts loved Batting Average. New school "Moneyball" GMs prefer OPS (On-base Plus Slugging) because it accounts for walks and power. A single is not the same value as a Home Run, but Batting Average treats them equally.

Why this matters

Stats don't lie. They tell you if a player is actually contributing or just getting lucky. Whether for fantasy leagues or contract negotiations, the Average is the first number everyone looks at.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does a Sacrifice Fly lower my average? In Baseball: No. A Sac Fly counts as an RBI but not an At Bat. In Cricket: No, you aren't out, but you didn't score. It doesn't affect calculations unless you get out.

What is a "Good" High School average? Usually .400+. High school pitching is slower, so hitting standards are higher.

Why is Cricket average so much higher? Because in Cricket, you can score 100 runs in a single turn. In Baseball, the max you can do is 4 bases (1 run) per turn.
