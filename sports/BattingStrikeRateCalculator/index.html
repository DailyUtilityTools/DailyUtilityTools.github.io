About Batting Strike Rate

Batting strike rate is a measure of how quickly a batsman scores runs. It's calculated as runs scored per 100 balls faced. A higher strike rate indicates aggressive batting, which is especially valued in limited-overs cricket formats like T20 and ODI.

Formula

Strike Rate = (Runs Scored / Balls Faced) Ã— 100

Format-wise Standards

  • T20: 130+ is excellent, 150+ is explosive
  • ODI: 90+ is good, 100+ is excellent
  • Test: 50+ is good for aggressive players

Notable Strike Rates

  • Andre Russell (T20): 180+ career strike rate
  • AB de Villiers (ODI): 101.09 career strike rate
  • Virat Kohli (ODI): 93+ career strike rate
