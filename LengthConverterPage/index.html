Expert Insight

Machinist Talk: In precision engineering (like making car engines), people often talk in "mils" or "thou."

  • 1 Mil = 0.001 inches (one thousandth of an inch).
  • It is NOT a millimeter! A millimeter is way bigger. Don't mix them up, or your engine will explode.
