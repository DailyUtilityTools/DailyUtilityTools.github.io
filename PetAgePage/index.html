The Formula

The "7 years" rule is a myth.

  • First Year: Your 1-year-old dog is actually like a 15-year-old human (Teenager).
  • Second Year: Plays out about 9 human years (Young Adult, 24).
  • After that: It depends on size. Small dogs add ~4 years/year. Giant dogs add ~7 years/year.

Unlike your vet's chart, here's what that means

  • Small Dog (Chihuahua): Lives forever out of spite. At age 10, they are only 56.
  • Giant Dog (Great Dane): Gentle giants. At age 10, they are nearly 80. Hug them tight.
  • Cats: They basically stop aging after 2 and just become immortal spirits until age 15.
