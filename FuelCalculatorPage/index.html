Frequently Asked Questions

Is "Premium" gas worth it? Only if your car requires it. Putting premium in a regular Civic does not improve MPG or power. It just burns money.

What is MPGe? "Miles Per Gallon Equivalent." It allows electric cars (EVs) to be compared to gas cars. 33.7 kWh of electricity contains the same energy as 1 gallon of gas. A Tesla Model 3 gets roughly 130 MPGe.

Does AC waste gas? Yes, but at highway speeds, opening the windows creates so much "drag" that using AC is actually more efficient.
