The Formula

The FLAMES algorithm:

  1. Take two names (e.g., "Kylie" and "Timothee").
  2. Cross out common letters (K, y, l, i, e / T, i, m, o, t, h, e, e -> i, e cancel out).
  3. Count the left over letters.
  4. Count through the acronym F-L-A-M-E-S until one letter remains.
