Why this matters

Your period is a vital sign. Missing periods (Amenorrhea) can signal stress, low body fat, or PCOS. Tracking helps you advocate for your health.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is 21 days normal? Yes. Anything from 21 to 35 days is considered "normal."

Can stress delay it? Absolutely. Cortisol (stress hormone) steals resources from Progesterone (period hormone). High stress = Late period.

Does birth control fix irregularity? It "masks" it. The pill gives you a "withdrawal bleed," not a real period. It forces regularity but doesn't fix the underlying cause.
