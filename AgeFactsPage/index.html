Why this matters

Humans are bad at visualizing long periods of time. Realizing you only have ~4,000 weeks in an average life (check out Memento Mori) sounds scary, but it's actually motivating. It reminds you to stop scrolling and go do something cool with the ~1,000 minutes you have today.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the heart rate exact? It's an estimate. We use the global average of 72 - 80 beats per minute. If you're an elite athlete, your total might be lower (efficient pump). If you drink 8 coffees a day, it might be higher!

How do you know how much I sleep? We assume the average human spends roughly one-third of their life asleep. If you're 30, you've likely slept through 10 full years of it.

What about Leap Years? Our calculator includes the extra day every 4 years ($365.25$ days/year), so your "Total Days" count is accurate.

What is a "Golden Birthday"? A Golden Birthday is when you turn the age of your birth date. Example: Turning 25 on the 25th of the month. It only happens once in a lifetime!
